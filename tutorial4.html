How to handle jetlag

  • Make sure you are well rested before the trip.
  • When you arrive, if you are a coffee drinker, have your normal amount, but don't overdo it.
  • Get outside and exercise. Walk around, see the sights, go shopping. Keeping moving will help.
  • Make yourself stay awake as long as you can, don't nap. Go to bed at close to your normal bedtime by the local time.
