Ireland - Car

Driving on the other side of the street

Driving on the other side of the road

In Ireland and other European countries, your steering wheel is on the right hand side, and you drive on the left side of the road.

Stick shifts are more common in Europe and cost significantly less than automatics to rent. The stick shift will be on the left, which takes some getting used to.

Anywhere - try the train tours

Travelling by train

If you get a chance to take a train tour, do it!

Many towns have train or bus tours.
