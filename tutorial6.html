Simplify!

Men - try to simplify and cut down on what you need to carry. Use travel size shampoo, toothpaste, etc. Most Hotels and Bed and Breakfasts will provide a blowdryer.

Women - this would be the time to have a chip proof manicure done. Simplify your makeup routine. Don't bring a curling iron or blowdryer if heading to Europe, yours won't plug in there. It is safer to buy a cheap curling iron there if you need one.
